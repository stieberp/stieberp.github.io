I've had issues with this print sometimes failing. Keep an eye on it.

After the print, you're going to need to a couple of threaded inserts.

I bought the following from https://www.mcmaster.com/ I already had a soldering iron.

Installation Tip for 10-32/10-24/M5 Thread Size Heat-Set Inserts for Plastics 92160A127
Tapered Heat-Set Inserts for Plastic Brass, M5 x 0.80 mm Thread Size, 6.700 mm Installed Length 94180A361

Get your ass to a well ventilated area. Install the special tip into the soldering iron. Heat up the inserts, and set them flush into the little holes at the front of the stock.

Now disassemble a g3 stock assembly, and reasemble with the new stock. Mount a red dot, or scope with an ar-15 height mount, and have fun!

This works for hk-91 pattern rifles. If you have a cetme c pattern rifle, what you can do is buy a surplus g3 stock assembly, and carefully sand down the plastic ring on the recoil spring until it fits in the cetme carrier.
